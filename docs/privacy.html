Privacy Policy

Tuitionlog is an offline-first app. All of your data — entries, notes, and settings — is stored only in your device's local app storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or identifiers. The app makes no network requests except those required by Apple's StoreKit to process optional in-app purchases, which are handled entirely by Apple and are governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Because nothing leaves your device, there is no account, no server, and no way for us to access your information.
